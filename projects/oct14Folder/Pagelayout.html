Michael Jordan is without a doubt the best basketball player of all time. He won 6 NBA Championship, and receive 5 MVPs. His extraordinary talent on the court led the bulls to win 1991, 1992, 1993, 1996, 1997, and 1998 NBA Championship. that caught Nike's attention for a collaboration of sneakers with him. The first Air Jordan sneaker that was been release in the year 1985 is the Air Jordan 1. His first signature shoes caught a lot of attention in the public and even up until now, is still one of the best shoes of 2024.

Jordan 1 made a collaboration with Travis Scott. Travis Scott is a famous American rapper and singer-songwriter. Nike and travis Scott made a collaboration that led to shoes that can cost upto 2,500 USD in resell price in some online stores such as Stock X and Goat. The unique design of each of the sneakers and its quality is what makes it expensive especially being limited pairs. Jordan 1 x Travis Scott Medium Olives is the recent release that have today that cost 150USD in retail price.
